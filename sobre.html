Our Journey

1988

Formation in Sacramento by Chino Moreno, Steph Carpenter & Abe Cunningham.

1995 – Adrenaline

Debut album. Raw energy and a cult following begins.

1997 – Around the Fur

Breakthrough success with “My Own Summer” and “Be Quiet and Drive”.

2000 – White Pony

Grammy-winning masterpiece. Experimental sound, cult-defining release.

2003 – Deftones

Darker and heavier, exploring new sonic territories.

2006 – Saturday Night Wrist

Emotionally intense and experimental, despite turbulent sessions.

2008 – Chi Cheng Accident

Bassist Chi falls into a coma after a car crash. Album “Eros” put on hold.

2010 – Diamond Eyes

A rebirth with Sergio Vega. Heavy yet uplifting sound, a new chapter.

2012 – Koi No Yokan

Critically acclaimed. Atmospheric and crushing melodies.

2013 – RIP Chi Cheng

After years in a coma, Chi passes away. The band and fans mourn worldwide.

2016 – Gore

More experimental, ethereal textures, expanding the sonic palette.

2020 – Ohms

A return to heaviness, praised for balancing aggression and atmosphere.

2025 – Private Music

Their latest release. A bold evolution, blending crushing riffs with haunting atmospheres, solidifying Deftones’ legacy for a new generation.

The Journey Continues...

From raw beginnings to timeless masterpieces, Deftones remain pioneers of alternative metal — forever pushing boundaries, forever captivating hearts.

“Music to Lose Yourself In”

Band Members

Chino Moreno

Lead vocals / Rhythm guitar. Founding member and the voice of Deftones, blending aggressive screams with haunting melodies.

Stephen Carpenter

Lead guitar. Known for crushing riffs and experimenting with extended-range guitars to push the band’s sound further.

Abe Cunningham

Drums. His dynamic playing blends groove and power, driving the band’s rhythm since the beginning.

Frank Delgado

Keyboards / Turntables / Samples. Introduced atmospheric textures and electronic elements into Deftones’ signature sound.

Fred Sablan

Bass / Backing vocals. The newest permanent member, bringing raw energy and solid low-end groove to the lineup.
